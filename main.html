About the Curator

YANJUN SHEN

Yanjun Shen is currently a graduate student in Migration and Diaspora Studies at Carleton University. She is particularly interested in Canadian cinema and films that engage with identity, migration, and politics.

She is also the creator and host of Film Unspoken, a bilingual film podcast.
